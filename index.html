About The Kingdom of Hyrule

Kingdom of Legend

The kingdom of Hyrule shows its rich history through its unique towns. From the cultural richness of Kakariko Village to the serene beauty of Hateno, the watery haven of Zora's Domain, the fiery energy of Goron City, the aerial wonders of Rito Village, the bustling activity of Gerudo Town, and the laid-back coastal charm of Lurelin Village, each town shows a unique tale, contributing to the vibrant tapestry of this land.
